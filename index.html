T-Halow Config

Configure a LilyGo T-Halow radio (Taixin TX-AH) over its USB serial console. Nothing is installed; nothing leaves your browser.

Web Serial unavailable. This page needs Chrome, Edge or Opera on desktop. Firefox and Safari do not implement Web Serial, and it is not available on Android. It also requires a secure context — use https:// or http://localhost.
disconnected

The P4's console multiplexes the ESP32's own log output with the radio's AT UART, so replies arrive interleaved with noise. Connecting quietens the radio's periodic status dump (AT+SYSDBG=LMAC,0) and resynchronises the console before talking to it.

Configure

Leave the factory reset on. Reconfiguring without it frequently leaves the two ends unable to associate, with no error on either side — the stored config is applied inconsistently. It costs about 12 seconds.
